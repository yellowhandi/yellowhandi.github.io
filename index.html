Welcome To

Yellow Handi | Exquisite Indian Cuisine

Welcome to Yellow Handi, a premier Indian restaurant nestled in the heart of the USA. With a name inspired by the traditional clay cooking pot used in Indian cuisine, we bring you an authentic and unforgettable dining experience. Our talented chefs meticulously craft each dish, using only the freshest ingredients and a harmonious blend of spices.

From classic favorites like butter chicken and biryani to regional specialties, our menu offers a tantalizing journey through the flavors of India.

We pride ourselves on our warm hospitality and inviting atmosphere, making Yellow Handi the perfect destination for a memorable meal with family and friends. Come and join us as we take you on a culinary adventure to the vibrant and diverse land of India.
