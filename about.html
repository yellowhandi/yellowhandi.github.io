Welcome To Yello Handi

Delectable Cuisine, Welcoming Team, Intimate Ambiance & Uplifting Vibes!

Welcome to Yellow Handi, where passion for Indian cuisine and a love for hospitality come together to create an unforgettable dining experience.With deep roots in India's diverse culinary heritage, our founders embarked on a mission to craft a menu that honors tradition while embracing creativity.Our chefs, trained in the art of Indian cooking, pour their heart and soul into every dish. We source the finest local ingredients and spices to ensure that each bite bursts with flavor and authenticity. Whether you're craving the fiery spices of the North, the aromatic curries of the South, or the delicate sweets of the East, our menu offers a gastronomic journey across India. But we are more than just a restaurant. We are a family, and our guests are an integral part of it. From the moment you step through our doors, you become part of our story. Our dedicated team strives to make your visit special, ensuring you feel the warmth and hospitality that India is renowned for.

Our Mission

Our mission is to consistently deliver an unparalleled dining experience by offering a diverse menu of exquisite Indian dishes prepared with precision, using high-quality ingredients, and served with genuine warmth. We are committed to preserving the essence of Indian culinary traditions while embracing innovation to cater to the evolving tastes of our patrons.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the ultimate destination for authentic Indian cuisine, where the vibrant tapestry of flavors, aromas, and culture of India come alive on every plate. We aspire to delight our guests with a culinary voyage that evokes nostalgia, celebrates tradition, and ignites a lifelong love affair with Indian food

Yellow Handi is known for creating unforgettable food experiences. We believe that food is therapy and try to put up a smile on your face with our culinary skills.
